Jimin's Solo Album FACE

Released in March 2023, FACE is Jimin’s first solo album. A deeply personal project exploring identity, vulnerability, and emotional reflection.

FACE is Jimin’s debut solo album, showcasing his artistic identity, emotions, and global impact. The album includes “Like Crazy,” which made history on the Billboard Hot 100.

With the global hit Like Crazy, Jimin became the first Korean solo artist to debut at #1 on the Billboard Hot 100, marking a historic moment in music.

"Set Me Free Pt. 2" is a pre-release track (first digital single) from Jimin's debut solo album "FACE". It was released on March 17, 2023 at 1 PM KST through Big Hit Music, two weeks before the full album "FACE" was released on March 24, 2023.

TITLE TRACK

"Like Crazy" is the title track from Jimin (BTS)'s first solo album, FACE, released on March 24, 2023. The song is an electronic pop song with dance-pop and synth-pop influences. Its lyrics depict a "crazy" love obsession, the feeling of being unable to let go of someone, and a dark yet catchy theme. The music video is incredibly dramatic, featuring an intense performance concept, stunning choreography, and Jimin's mesmerizing visuals! The song instantly became a global phenomenon and broke numerous records, especially as Jimin's incredibly successful solo debut. Here are the full achievements and key records for "Like Crazy" (based on data through early 2026, and the song continues to chart and stream highly)

“Like Crazy” debuted at #1 on the Billboard Hot 100, making Jimin the first Korean solo artist in history to achieve this milestone.

Main Chart Achievements

  • Billboard Hot 100 (US): Debuts straight at No. 1 (chart date April 8, 2023) → First time in the Hot 100's 65+ year history that a South Korean solo artist has reached No. 1. Also No. 1 on the Billboard Hot 100. First No. 1 for a BTS member as a solo artist.
  • Billboard Global 200 & Global Excl. US: Entered and held a long-standing position, part of Jimin's milestone of having two songs (Like Crazy + Who) chart for over 50 weeks on both global charts (a first for a K-pop soloist).
  • Spotify Global: Was No. 1 (a first for a Korean solo artist), and continues to be one of the most iconic K-pop songs.
  • Other charts: Top 10 in the UK Singles (debuted No. 8, a record high for a Korean solo artist), as well as in numerous countries such as Hong Kong, Indonesia, Malaysia, etc.

Streaming Record (Spotify)

  • Surpassed 1 billion streams in April 2024 (377 days after release) → First for a Korean solo song.
  • Fastest Korean song to reach 800 million streams (in less than a year).
  • By 2025-2026: Over 1.4 billion streams (still growing, including a significant contribution to Jimin's total billion streams).
  • Contributed to Jimin becoming the K-pop soloist with the most streams without collaborations.

Certifications & Sales

  • RIAA (US): Certified Platinum (1 million+ units sold/equivalent, including sales + streams).
  • UK (BPI): Certified Silver (2025).
  • Canada: Platinum (80,000+ units).
  • Very high first-week debut sales (254,000+ downloads/CD singles in the US alone, a 2023 record for a solo song).

Other Achievements

  • YouTube MV: Over 100 million views (October 2023).
  • iTunes — #1 on Top Songs chart in all countries.
  • Contributed to the smash hit album "FACE" (No. 2 debut on the Billboard 200).
  • Still relevant in 2026: Frequently appearing on Spotify's US/global daily charts, part of Jimin's legacy as a "history maker" on Billboard and Spotify.

Awards & recognitions

Made a "Most Performed Song" list at the BMI Pop Awards, and consistently mentioned in annual recaps as one of the most impactful K-pop songs post-2023.

In short, "Like Crazy" wasn't just a hit, but a game-changer for Jimin's solo career and proved the power of solo K-pop in the global market.

FACE ALBUM

BTS Jimin's debut solo album, FACE, was released on March 24, 2023. This album is a deeply personal project for Jimin, in which he was directly involved in the songwriting and conceptualization.

Album Description & Meaning

  • Main Theme: This album explores the honest emotions Jimin felt during the COVID-19 pandemic, including loneliness, inner struggles, and self-discovery.
  • Visual Concept: Carrying two main themes, namely "Invisible Face" (representing his pure and vulnerable side) and "Undefinable Face" (showing his more intense and bold side).
  • Tracklist: Consists of six songs spanning various genres:
  • 1. Face-off: About facing the harsh reality of betrayal.
  • 2. Interlude: Dive: An instrumental that transitions into Jimin's emotional world.
  • 3. Like Crazy (Title Track): Inspired by the film of the same name, it depicts the desire to remain in a dream to escape reality.
  • 4. Alone: A ballad about feelings of isolation and emptiness.
  • 5. Set Me Free Pt. 2: A powerful pre-release track about breaking free from pain and limitations.
  • 6. Like Crazy (English Version).
  • 7. The hidden track in Jimin's solo debut album, FACE, is titled "Letter" (편지) or often called "Dear. ARMY".

List of Key Achievements

FACE's album made history as the first K-Pop soloist to chart on multiple platforms:

1. Billboard (United States)

  • Hot 100 History: The single "Like Crazy" became the first solo song by a South Korean artist to reach #1 on the Billboard Hot 100.
  • Billboard 200: The album debuted at #2, making it the highest-charting Korean soloist on the chart at the time of its release.
  • Artist 100: Jimin became the first Korean soloist to reach #1 on the Artist 100 chart.
  • World Albums: The longest-charting album by a K-Pop soloist (over 56 weeks).

2. Sales & Streaming

  • Hanteo (Korea): First soloist in Hanteo history to sell over 1 million copies on the first day of release.
  • Spotify: First album by a Korean soloist to surpass 1 billion streams on Spotify.
  • iTunes: Reached #1 on iTunes charts in over 100 countries.

3. Awards & World Records

  • Guinness World Records: Jimin broke the record for the fastest male solo artist to reach 1 billion streams on Spotify.
  • MAMA Awards 2023: Won the Best Male Artist award for the success of this album.

FACE became the first album by a Korean soloist to surpass 1 billion streams on Spotify, highlighting Jimin’s global influence.

The FACE vinyl was released gradually across different regions from late 2023 to early 2024. Below are the details of its release schedule and achievements.

FACE Vinyl Release Schedule

  • Official Release & Shipping:
  • South Korea: December 8, 2023
  • Japan: December 15, 2023

FACE Vinyl Achievements

Although vinyl editions are usually released as collector versions after the digital/CD album, the FACE vinyl made a significant contribution to the album’s long-term success.

  • Billboard Chart Re-entry: Thanks to the physical vinyl release in January 2024, the album FACE re-entered several major Billboard charts, including the Billboard 200 and the Vinyl Albums Chart.
  • High Position on Vinyl Albums Chart: In the United States, the vinyl reached the Top 10 (peaking at #2) on the weekly Billboard Vinyl Albums Chart during its release week.
  • Strong Physical Sales: The vinyl added to the album's traditional sales, helping FACE remain one of the best-selling solo K-Pop albums in physical format.
  • Certification Contribution: Vinyl sales contributed to the album reaching major certifications including Million certification from KMCA (Korea) and Platinum certification from RIAJ (Japan).
