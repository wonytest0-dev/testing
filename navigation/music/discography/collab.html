ANGEL PT.2 2023

"Angel Pt. 2" by JVKE featuring Jimin of BTS, Charlie Puth, and Muni Long was released on June 15, 2023 as the emotional sequel to "Angel Pt. 1" from the Fast X soundtrack. Unlike the trap-driven first part, this version highlights a softer piano-driven pop/R&B style with powerful vocal harmonies.

ACHIEVEMENT:
  • Showcased Jimin's versatility in emotional pop/R&B ballads.
  • Follow-up soundtrack appearance after "Angel Pt. 1".
  • Celebrated as Jimin's 15th song to surpass 100M Spotify streams.
  • Official lyric video reached 11.5M+ YouTube views.
  • Continues growing in streams years after release.
  • Considered a sleeper streaming hit among Jimin collaborations.

ANGEL PT.1 2023

"Angel Pt. 1" by NLE Choppa and Kodak Black featuring Jimin of BTS, JVKE, and Muni Long was released on May 18, 2023 as part of the Fast X soundtrack. The track blends trap, hip-hop, and emotional vocals, with Jimin delivering the memorable chorus that helped the song stand out globally.

ACHIEVEMENT:
  • Debuted at #65 on the Billboard Hot 100 (June 3, 2023).
  • Jimin's fourth solo Hot 100 entry within a short period.
  • Highest-peaking OST entry for a Korean act on the Hot 100 at the time.
  • Billboard Global 200 peak: #16.
  • Canada Hot 100 peak: #63.
  • US Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs peak: #18.
  • US Pop Airplay peak: #35.
  • UK Singles Chart peak: #82.
  • Strong Spotify debut (#23 with 2.6M+ streams).
  • Helped cement Jimin as the fastest K-act with four Hot 100 debuts in 2023.

VIBE 2023

"Vibe" by TAEYANG featuring Jimin of BTS, released in January 2023, marked a major moment in Jimin's solo career as his first high-profile global collaboration outside BTS. The R&B-influenced track highlighted Jimin's distinctive vocal tone alongside Taeyang's signature style.

ACHIEVEMENT:
  • Debuted at #76 on the Billboard Hot 100 (January 28, 2023).
  • First solo Hot 100 entry for both Taeyang and Jimin.
  • First Hot 100 entry for a Korean soloist in 2023.
  • Marked Jimin as the seventh BTS member to chart solo.
  • #1 on Circle Global Daily K-pop Chart.
  • High rankings on Spotify Global, Melon, and YouTube charts.
  • Won 1st place on M Countdown (January 19, 2023).
  • Massive iTunes success with 100 #1s worldwide.
  • Nominated for Best Music (Spring) at The Fact Music Awards.

WITH YOU 2022

"With You" is Jimin's emotional OST collaboration with Ha Sung-woon for the drama Our Blues. The song highlights Jimin's soft vocal tone and emotional storytelling, becoming one of the most beloved drama soundtracks connected to his discography.

ACHIEVEMENT:
  • Surpassed 300 million Spotify streams by 2025 and continues to grow.
  • Fastest song ever at the time to reach #1 in 100 countries on iTunes (4h 42m).
  • Achieved global iTunes All-Kill across 119 regions.
  • Debuted #2 then climbed to #1 on Billboard Digital Song Sales.
  • Appeared on Billboard Global 200 and Global Excl. US charts.
  • Nominated for Best OST and Song of the Year at MAMA 2022.
  • Won Collaboration of the Year at Dabeme Music Awards 2023.
  • Over 710,000 global Shazam identifications as of 2026.

CHRISTMAS LOVE 2020

"Christmas Love" by Jimin (of BTS) is a heartfelt holiday track he self-composed and surprise-released on SoundCloud on December 24, 2020, inspired by his childhood Christmas memories. It was officially released on major streaming platforms on March 6, 2023 alongside his solo track "Promise".

ACHIEVEMENT:
  • Reached millions of views rapidly (2M in ~4 hours, 7M+ in the first day).
  • Became the most-streamed Korean audio, solo Korean audio, and Korean male audio on YouTube within 24 hours.
  • Surpassed 10M views quickly — a record for Korean audio at the time.
  • Charted on YouTube Music in 71+ countries with strong global rankings.
  • Over 9M SoundCloud streams shortly after release, trending worldwide.
  • Achieved #1 on iTunes in numerous countries (31+ reported in 2023).
  • Charted on global holiday charts including top Christmas songs in several countries.

FILTER 2020

Jimin's solo track "Filter" from BTS's album Map of the Soul: 7 (2020) became one of his most successful non-title solo songs. The track showcases his versatility, Latin pop-inspired sound, and themes of identity and self-reinvention. Despite not being an official single, "Filter" achieved massive global streaming success and remains one of Jimin’s most recognizable solo performances.

ACHIEVEMENT:
  • Surpassed 500 million Spotify streams (January 6, 2026) — the most-streamed BTS solo track on the platform.
  • Ranks as the third most-played song from Map of the Soul: 7 overall.
  • Earlier milestone: 450+ million streams showing steady global growth.
  • Official audio video on YouTube surpassed 131–132 million views.
  • Melon streams: 136.6 million — the most-streamed BTS solo song on the platform.
  • Over 620,000 Shazam searches globally.
  • Historic global iTunes all-kill in July 2024 across 119 countries.
  • Reached top 50 on US iTunes Top Songs (#41 peak).
  • #1 on iTunes in up to 75 countries during peak fan-driven surges.
  • Recognized for its Latin pop sound and Jimin's vocal versatility.

FRIENDS 2020

Jimin's "Friends" (친구) is a heartfelt duet with V (Taehyung) from BTS's album Map of the Soul: 7 (released February 21, 2020). Jimin co-wrote the lyrics (with RM and others), and it's a nostalgic acoustic-pop track reflecting their trainee days friendship—often called one of the most beautiful subunit songs in BTS discography.

ACHIEVEMENT:
  • One of the enduring fan favorites from Map of the Soul: 7 with hundreds of millions of Spotify streams.
  • Contributed to Jimin surpassing 6.6B Spotify streams across solo credits.
  • Frequently appears on global charts including Deezer Argentina and Apple Music Belarus.
  • Part of the Map of the Soul: 7 era known for incredible longevity on streaming platforms.
  • Selected for Marvel's Eternals (2021) soundtrack, giving global exposure.
  • Often called "Vmin's promise" by fans due to its emotional message.
  • Remains a powerful friendship anthem even 6+ years after release.

PROMISE 2018

Jimin's "Promise" (약속) is BTS member Jimin's first self-composed solo song (lyrics co-written with RM, produced with Slow Rabbit). It was released for free on December 30/31, 2018 as a heartfelt New Year’s gift to fans (ARMY) on BTS’s SoundCloud page — no promotion, just pure love. Official release came later on March 6, 2023.

ACHIEVEMENT:
  • Biggest 24-hour debut ever on SoundCloud (Guinness World Record): 8.5M streams in 24 hours.
  • Broke Drake’s previous record (4.9M) and topped SoundCloud charts for 48+ hours.
  • Most-streamed song of all time on SoundCloud (Guinness World Record).
  • First song ever to surpass 300M streams (December 2021).
  • 330M+ streams by June 17, 2023 → certified by Guinness on August 2, 2023.
  • Still the #1 streamed track in SoundCloud history as of 2026.
  • #1 on iTunes Top Songs in 51+ countries.
  • 100,000+ units sold in the US (RIAA-eligible).

SERENDIPITY 2017

Serendipity (Intro: Serendipity / Serendipity Full Length Edition) is Jimin’s iconic solo track by BTS. The Intro version dropped as the comeback trailer for Love Yourself: Her (September 2017), and the Full Length Edition appeared on Love Yourself: Answer (August 2018). It’s a dreamy, sensual R&B song with Jimin showcasing his vocal range and emotional depth.

ACHIEVEMENT:
  • The comeback trailer MV was the first BTS member solo music video to reach 100 million views (May 2019).
  • Full Length Edition surpassed 300 million streams (April 2025).
  • Combined versions hit 200M+ streams in early 2021.
  • RIAA Gold eligible (500,000+ units) for the Full Length Edition (March 2022).
  • Jimin became the first K-pop idol with two RIAA Gold solo songs (Lie + Serendipity).
  • Intro version peaked #2 Billboard World Digital Song Sales.
  • Full Length peaked #5 Billboard World Digital Song Sales.
  • Highest-charting non-title track on Korean streaming platforms.
  • First BTS solo track to appear on the iTunes Worldwide Top 100.

LIE 2016

"Lie" by Jimin (from BTS' 2016 album Wings) is one of the most iconic and enduring solo tracks in K-pop. Released on October 10, 2016 as Jimin's first solo song — co-written by him — it achieved major milestones in streams, sales, and certifications worldwide.

ACHIEVEMENT:
  • 60M streams – first BTS solo song to reach this milestone (2019)
  • 100M streams – first Wings B-side to do so (2020)
  • One of the most streamed BTS solo/sub-unit tracks
  • 500,000+ units sold in the United States
  • Gold certification in Canada
  • #4 Billboard World Digital Song Sales
  • One of the longest charting solo tracks from Wings
