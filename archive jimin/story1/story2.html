They say that since childhood, Jimin has had an aura that makes people fall in love easily. His face is so sweet that the neighbors consider him their little mascot, the pride of their neighborhood.

Making BTS Jimin look cute 🐥✨ (feat. his Geumsa-dong childhood days)

Everyone knows Jimin as a global superstar, but in my memories, the Jimin from the Geumsa-dong days was just a tiny little kid who waddled around like a baby haha.

Back when I was in college, my older sister and my mom were the type who were extremely weak when it came to pretty faces.

Whenever I was busy playing games, that tiny kid would come toddling over and stare at my monitor with so much focus.

Wherever he went, he was basically the cute mascot of the neighborhood, loved by everyone around him.

Honestly, these days whenever I see him on TV, I feel a kind of happy regret. If I had known he would grow up to become someone this huge who would sweep the entire world, I would’ve taken photos of him like crazy back then!

Still, whenever I see how he makes so many people passionate and excited, my heart fills up with pride like it’s my own achievement.

Jimin is already receiving all the love in the world. From now on, I hope he continues to shine even brighter.
