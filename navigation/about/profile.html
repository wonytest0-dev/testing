PARK JIMIN

Park Jimin (박지민), known simply as Jimin, is a South Korean singer and performer celebrated worldwide as a member of BTS. Born in Busan on October 13, 1995, he trained in modern dance before debuting and quickly became known for his expressive artistry, graceful movement, and distinctive voice.

Blending elegance with emotional depth, Jimin’s performances carry a rare sensitivity that connects deeply with audiences around the world. Both on stage and beyond it, he is admired not only for his artistry, but also for his warmth, sincerity, and compassionate heart.

JIMIN

THE ARTIST

Park Jimin, known as Jimin of BTS, is a globally recognized artist admired for his artistry, stage presence, and emotional performances. This profile explores his background, personality, and impact as one of the most influential K-pop idols.

Renowned for his ethereal vocals and extraordinary dance artistry, Jimin has captivated audiences across the globe. His performances blend elegance, emotion, and power, creating moments that resonate far beyond the stage.

With his authenticity, warmth, and unwavering dedication to his craft, Jimin continues to inspire millions of fans around the world.

THE PERFORMANCE MASTER

Jimin is often called the "Stage Commander." He began dancing at a young age, mastering hip-hop, popping, and locking, before honing his craft at Busan Arts High School (부산예술고등학교), where he majored in modern contemporary dance. This rare combination of street-style foundations and formal contemporary training allows him to fuse genres in a way few performers can—his movement is grounded yet fluid, sharp yet lyrical, creating a style entirely his own.

As BTS’s main dancer and a solo performer, Jimin brings this hybrid style to every stage, captivating audiences with choreography that tells a story. Whether it’s his legendary solo “Lie,” the breathtaking “Serendipity,” the sultry, dreamlike storytelling of “Like Crazy,” the emotionally magnetic “Who,” or his modern masterpiece “Set Me Free Pt.2”, Jimin doesn’t just dance—he creates a language of movement that speaks directly to the heart.

THE ETHEREAL VOICE

His vocal range is nothing short of magical. From delicate, sweet falsettos to powerful, raspy belts, Jimin’s voice is one of the most distinctive in the industry—capable of conveying both fragility and intensity with remarkable emotional depth. As BTS’s lead vocalist, his ethereal tone elevates every song he performs, whether as a soloist or as part of the group.

One of Jimin’s defining artistic strengths is his genre-defying musical identity. Rather than being confined to a single style, he moves effortlessly between different genres, infusing each with his distinct emotional expression. Fans and critics alike often describe his music as uniquely “Jimin’s genre” — a reflection of his versatility, creativity, and artistry without boundaries.

THE COMPASSIONATE HEART

Beyond the stage lights, Jimin is celebrated for his warm heart, empathy, and generosity. Often described by fans as the heart of BTS, he is the “glue” that brings the members together—known for his kindness and his ability to comfort those around him.

His compassion extends far beyond his group. Affectionately called the “Charity Fairy”, Jimin lives by his own belief to “Be a good human,” supporting numerous charitable causes and inspiring fans worldwide to follow his example. His bright smile, affectionate personality, and authentic presence are just a few of the reasons he is deeply loved by both his members and fans across the globe.

RECORD-BREAKING SOLOIST

As a solo artist, Jimin has achieved historic milestones on the global stage. His debut solo album, FACE, was a deeply personal project, reflecting his emotions, experiences, and artistic identity. The title track “Like Crazy” debuted at #1 on the Billboard Hot 100, making him the first Korean solo artist to achieve this remarkable feat.

His solo song “Who” further cemented his legacy, remaining on the Hot 100 for 33 weeks, surpassing even the records set by BTS. Across streaming platforms and charts worldwide, Jimin continues to redefine what it means to be a modern pop icon, proving that his talent transcends borders.

GLOBAL INFLUENCE

With his immense cultural influence and worldwide popularity, Jimin is often referred to by fans as the “Global IT Boy,” reflecting his impact across music, fashion, and pop culture.

He serves as a global ambassador for luxury brands such as Dior and Tiffany & Co., and was newly announced as a brand ambassador for Lador in December 2025.

His influence extends into measurable brand perception as well: Jimin has consistently ranked #1 on Korea’s Brand Reputation Index for individual idols and boy group members, achieving one of the most sustained top positions in the ranking’s history.

This remarkable achievement underscores his broad public appeal, global recognition, and the powerful cultural influence he continues to hold across the entertainment and fashion industries.

LEGACY AND INSPIRATION

Whether through his breathtaking performances, ethereal voice, or the warmth and empathy he brings to his group and fans alike, Jimin has established himself as more than an artist—he is a cultural force whose influence transcends music.

His record-breaking achievements as a soloist and his lasting impact on global pop culture are a testament to his talent, dedication, and the deep connection he shares with audiences worldwide.

With every stage, every note, and every gesture, Jimin continues to inspire, captivate, and define what it means to be a true icon of his generation.

JIMIN CHARITY

Jimin (Park Ji-min of BTS) is widely recognized for his extensive and consistent philanthropy, often conducted quietly and without seeking publicity. Nicknamed the "Charity Angel" or "Charity King" by fans and media, he focuses primarily on education, child welfare, youth support, and community aid in South Korea, while also contributing to global causes. His efforts have earned him spots on prestigious lists like South Korea's "The Givers 50" (honoring top donors) in recent years, and he, along with his father and younger brother, made history as the first family of three to join ChildFund Korea's Green Noble Club (a network of major individual donors) in 2025.

Education Scholarships: Since 2019, Jimin has donated 100 million KRW (approximately $70,000–$75,000 USD, depending on exchange rates) annually to provincial education offices across South Korea. He selects one region each year to support underprivileged students with scholarships, books, reading programs, school meals, and other needs. This pattern continued through at least 2025, marking his sixth consecutive year and totaling 600 million KRW (around $450,000 USD) across regions including:

Busan (hometown, 2019) Jeonnam/South Jeolla (2020) Chungbuk/North Chungcheong (2023 example) Gyeongnam/South Gyeongsang (2024) Jeonbuk/North Jeolla (2025)

Child Welfare and Youth Independence: Through ChildFund Korea (Green Umbrella Children's Foundation), Jimin has been a member of the Green Noble Club since 2021. His donations support programs for at-risk children, low-income families, housing improvements, and independence for youth leaving foster care (e.g., 100 million KRW in 2021 for Busan-based initiatives). He has also provided scholarships to students at Busan Arts High School (his alma mater area).

Military and Community Support (during his mandatory service period, roughly 2023–2025): Even while enlisted, Jimin continued giving, including:

Donations for soldiers' families (e.g., 100 million KRW in 2024/2025 for scholarships, living, and medical aid). 30 million KRW to the Busan Habitat Challenge for elderly living conditions. Induction into the Namgu Hometown Love Donor Hall of Fame (small donation recognized for consistent welfare contributions).

Global and Disaster Relief: In 2023, he donated 100 million KRW to aid children affected by the Turkey-Syria earthquakes.

Jimin's philanthropy extends through BTS's collective efforts, such as the LOVE MYSELF campaign with UNICEF (2017–2025), which raised over US$6.6 million to end violence against children, promote mental health, and support programs in dozens of countries. While this was a group initiative, Jimin actively participated in its messaging and promotion.His donations are often delivered privately (e.g., via family members), inspiring fans (ARMY) to organize matching fundraisers and charity drives in his name. In 2025, he was highlighted in "The Givers 50" for his decade-long consistent giving, and fans frequently celebrate him as a role model for quiet, sustained kindness.
