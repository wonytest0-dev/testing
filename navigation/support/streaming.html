+ Streams Equivalent to One Album Sale

On major charts like Billboard, streams are converted into album units based on the type of platform used.

1,250 streams from paid platforms = 1 album unit

3,750 streams from free platforms = 1 album unit

10 track purchases = 1 album unit

Premium streams have higher value than free streams, so using paid platforms helps support the artist more effectively.

Tip: Use premium streaming platforms whenever possible, and avoid looping excessively — stream naturally.

+ Streams Equivalent to One Song Sale

For song charts like Billboard Hot 100, streams are converted into units based on platform type.

125–150 premium streams ≈ 1 song unit

375 free streams ≈ 1 song unit

For Billboard Global 200:

200 premium streams = 1 track sale

900 ad-supported streams = 1 track sale

Tracking week runs from Friday to Thursday.

Streaming Platform Guide

Spotify Guide

DO

  • Use a premium account if possible
  • Search and stream from official profile
  • Use varied playlists
  • Turn off shuffle & autoplay
  • Interact naturally (skip, like, share)

DON'T

  • Do not loop the same track
  • Do not mute audio
  • Avoid using VPN
  • Avoid repetitive patterns

YouTube Guide

DO

  • Watch full video without skipping
  • Use 480p or higher quality
  • Keep volume above 50%
  • Like, comment, and share
  • Watch other videos in between

DON'T

  • Do not loop continuously
  • Do not use VPN or incognito
  • Do not mute audio
  • Avoid bot-like behavior

Apple Music Guide

DO

  • Use subscription or free trial
  • Search manually for songs
  • Add songs to your library
  • Use playlists with variety
  • Interact with the app naturally

DON'T

  • Do not loop tracks
  • Do not mute while streaming
  • Avoid repetitive streaming

Amazon Music Guide

DO

  • Log in with your account
  • Create your own playlists
  • Use fillers between streams
  • Share songs
  • Interact naturally

DON'T

  • Do not loop songs
  • Do not mute audio
  • Avoid bot behavior

Deezer Guide

DO

  • Stream from official artist page
  • Use playlists with fillers
  • Listen at least 30 seconds
  • Interact (like, share, skip)

DON'T

  • Do not use VPN
  • Do not loop excessively
  • Avoid repetitive streaming
