JIMIN ON RADIO

Supporting Jimin on radio is one of the most powerful ways to increase his global impact.

Radio requesting is important for an artist’s airplay because it helps influence the music played on radio stations, which in turn can impact the artist's exposure, chart performance, and overall success.

When listeners request a song, it signals to radio stations that the song is in demand. Stations often adjust their playlists based on audience interest, so more requests can lead to increased airplay.

Program directors and DJs decide what gets played on the radio, and listener requests can directly influence their choices. If a song receives multiple requests, it shows strong demand and increases play frequency.

Radio airplay is a major component of charts like Billboard Hot 100. The more frequently a song is played, the higher it can climb.

When listeners repeatedly request a song, it creates buzz and excitement. This encourages more people to stream and support the track.

Popularity can grow regionally and expand globally. A song requested in one country can spread across markets.

Radio stations aim to attract listeners. Playing requested songs boosts engagement and overall ratings.
