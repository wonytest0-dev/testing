JIMIN ACHIEVEMENTS

Celebrating Jimin’s outstanding achievements and global impact as an artist.

Jimin has continuously proven his influence in the global music industry through remarkable achievements, record-breaking milestones, and prestigious awards. His artistry, dedication, and connection with fans have allowed him to stand out as one of the most impactful artists of his generation.

From chart-topping songs to historic records on major platforms, Jimin’s success reflects both his talent and the unwavering support from fans worldwide. Each achievement marks a new chapter in his journey, showcasing his growth as a solo artist.

His accomplishments go beyond numbers — they represent cultural impact, global recognition, and the power of music to connect people across the world. Jimin continues to break boundaries and set new standards in the industry.

Explore Jimin’s achievements, awards, and milestones from debut to present, highlighting his global impact and record-breaking success as a solo artist.

AWARDS & ACHIEVEMENTS

2018

  • Oct 24 — Hwagwan Order of Cultural Merit National cultural honor awarded to members of BTS for promoting Korean culture globally at the Korean Popular Culture and Arts Awards.
  • #1 Most Preferred Idol in Gallup Korea 2018 (up from #7 in 2017). Jimin is the first idol to be ranked #1 for two consecutive years (2018 & 2019).
  • From January to May 2018, Jimin continued to win the Peeper x Billboard “Top K-Pop Artist – Individual” every month (the prize was donated to UNICEF in his name).
  • Dec 30 — Released the song Promise and broke the record for most streamed song in 24 hours on SoundCloud at that time.

2019

  • May — Plaque of Appreciation Presented by the Kim Baek-Bong Korean Fan Dance Conservation Society for Jimin’s performance of traditional Korean fan dance at the Melon Music Awards.
  • Released Serendipity becomes BTS's first solo song to reach 100 million views on YouTube.

2020

  • Oct 7 — James A. Van Fleet Award (with BTS) Presented by The Korea Society for strengthening U.S.–Korea cultural relations through music.
  • Feb 20 — Released Filter
  • It became the most-streamed K-pop solo song in 2020 and the most-streamed BTS B-side track overall.
  • Fastest Korean male solo song to reach 100 million streams on Spotify.
  • Jimin became the first and only Korean soloist to have three solo songs (Lie, Serendipity Full Version, and Filter) reach 100 million streams on Spotify at the time.
  • Debuted on the Billboard Hot 100 at #87 (alongside Jungkook's "ON" and "My Time," a BTS record at the time).

2022

  • Apr — Released With You
  • #1 on iTunes in over 100 countries.
  • Topped the Billboard Digital Song Sales chart.
  • Nominated for several awards, including the APAN Star Awards (Best Original Soundtrack) and MAMA 2022 (Best OST).
  • Dec — Winning “Best Main Dancer” at the 2022 Melon Music Awards (MMA)

2023

  • Mnet Asian Music Awards — Best Male Artist
  • Melon Music Awards — Top 10 Artist & Millions Top 10 (Like Crazy)
  • Asian Pop Music Awards - Top 20 Albums of the Year (Overseas) w Face
  • The Fact Music Awards - Idols Popularity Award
  • Asia Artist Awards — Best Musician
  • “Like Crazy” debuts #1 on the Billboard Hot 100
  • First Korean soloist to enter Top 2 on the Billboard 200
  • First Korean soloist to spend 7 consecutive weeks on Billboard’s Artist 100
  • First Korean soloist to spend 50 weeks on Billboard Global 200 (Like Crazy)
  • Spotify — Fastest Korean solo song to 300M streams (Like Crazy)
  • Spotify — 1B+ streams on album Face
  • Fastest Korean solo artist to reach 1B total Spotify streams (male)
  • Face is the first Korean solo album to reach 1 billion streams on Spotify (August 2023).
  • Like crazy reached 119 countries at #1 on iTunes
  • First and only korean soloist at #1 billboard artist 100
  • Face reached #1 on Japan's Oricon Album Chart
  • Face sold over 1.45 million copies in its first week in Korea
  • Highest charting album by South Korean Soloist on billboard 200

2024

  • MAMA Awards - Fan’s Choice of the Year (Daesang)
  • MAMA Awards - Fan’s Choice - Male
  • Seoul Music Awards - Main Award (Bonsang)
  • MTV EMA 2024: Best K-Pop
  • Hanteo Music Awards 2024: Artist of the Year
  • Asia Artist Awards (AAA) 2024: Best Musician
  • Spotify — MUSE surpasses 1B streams
  • First Korean soloist with two different albums over 1B streams
  • “Who” enters Spotify Global #1
  • “Who” #1 on Billboard Global 200

2025

  • “Who” becomes longest-charting K-pop solo song on Billboard Hot 100
  • 400 consecutive days on Spotify USA Daily Songs Chart
  • iHeartRadio Music Awards 2025: K-Pop Song of the Year
  • BMI Pop Awards 2025
  • Hanteo Music Awards 2025
  • Billboard 2025 Year-End: “Who” #57 Hot 100
  • MUSE #3 World Albums
  • Spotify: "Who" was one of the most-listened K-pop solo songs in 2025
